Xay dung mot minh (solo developer) mot ung dung nhu Locket la mot thu thach thu vi nhung cung kha nang vi ban phai dam nhan cung luc 3 vai tro: Backend Engineer, Mobile Developer (iOS/Android) va UI/UX Designer.

Duoi day la uoc tinh thoi gian dua tren cac giai doan thuc te, gia dinh ban da co kien thuc co ban ve Python va dang hoc them ve lap trinh Mobile:

Tong thoi gian uoc tinh: 6 - 10 tuan
Neu ban lam viec tap trung (khoang 15-20 gio/tuan), lo trinh se chia lam cac giai doan sau:

Giai doan 1: Thiet ke & Chuan bi (Tuan 1)
UI/UX: Thiet ke giao dien tren Figma (don gian thoi, vi la ban clone).

Database Schema: Thiet lap cac bang User, Friends, Photos, Notifications.

Setup: Khoi tao project FastAPI, ket noi Database, cau hinh Cloudinary (luu anh) va Firebase (gui push).

Giai doan 2: Phat trien Backend - Python/FastAPI (Tuan 2 - 3)
Day la phan ban dang tap trung.

Viet API Dang ky/Dang nhap (JWT).

API ket ban, tim kiem ban be.

Quan trong nhat: API Upload anh, xu ly nen anh bang Pillow va luu len Cloud.

Logic gui thong bao Real-time qua Firebase Admin SDK.

Giai doan 3: Phat trien Mobile App (Tuan 4 - 7)
Day la phan ton thoi gian nhat vi Locket song nho vao Widget.

Giao dien App chinh: Chup anh, danh sach ban be, lich su anh.

Widget Development: Day la phan kho nhat. Ban phai hoc cach code Widget (dung SwiftUI cho iOS hoac Jetpack Compose cho Android) de anh hien ngay ra man hinh chinh khi ban be gui toi.

Ket noi Mobile voi Backend API da viet o Tuan 2-3.

Giai doan 4: Testing & Fix Bug (Tuan 8+)
Kiem tra do tre (latency): Anh gui di mat bao lau thi hien len Widget?

Xu ly cac truong hop mang yeu, loi upload.

Dong goi ung dung.
